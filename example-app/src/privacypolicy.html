Health Data Privacy Policy

Data We Access

This app requests access to the following health data through Health Connect:

  • Steps - Daily step count data
  • Distance - Distance traveled
  • Calories - Calories burned
  • Heart Rate - Heart rate measurements
  • Sleep - Sleep session data

How We Use Your Data

Your health data is used solely to provide you with health insights and tracking features within this app. We do not share your health data with third parties.

Data Storage

Health data accessed through Health Connect remains on your device. We do not upload or store your health data on external servers.

Your Control

You can revoke this app's access to your health data at any time through the Health Connect app settings.

Contact

If you have questions about how we handle your health data, please contact us.
